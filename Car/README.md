# 🅿️ Parking Management System

## 🌟 Overview
The **Parking Management System** is an Object-Oriented C++ application engineered to handle the complexities of parking lot administration. It models vehicles, parking spots, and ticketing records to simulate a real-world parking environment, ensuring efficient allocation and tracking of spaces.

## ✨ Key Features

- **Vehicle Management**: Track cars, their license plates, and types.
- **Spot Allocation**: Dynamically assign vehicles to available parking spots and free up spots when vehicles leave.
- **Record Keeping**: Generate parking records detailing entry times, exit times, and fees.
- **Clean Architecture**: Separates models (`Car`, `ParkingSpot`, `ParkingRecord`), services (`ParkingSystem`), and UI (`ParkingScreen`) for high maintainability.

## 🛠️ Technical Highlights
- **Language**: C++
- **Design Focus**: Separation of Concerns, Encapsulation, and structured data flow.
- **Scalability**: Easily extendable to support different vehicle types (e.g., motorcycles, trucks) or dynamic pricing models.
